About BlockSafe

BlockSafe is a sustainable and cost-effective transportation solution that allows multiple passengers to share a single vehicle, reducing traffic congestion and carbon emissions. Blockchain technology plays a crucial role in carpooling by providing secure and transparent transactions, ensuring trust among participants, and enabling efficient management of ride-sharing data.
